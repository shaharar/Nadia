About Nadia


Nadia Comaneci is a Romanian retired gymnast and a five-time Olympic gold medalist, all in individual events.

She is the first gymnast to be awarded a perfect score of 10.0 at the Olympic Games (1976 Summer Olympics in Montreal).

During her career, Comaneci won nine Olympic medals and four World Artistic Gymnastics Championship medals.


Comaneci is one of the world's best-known gymnasts and is credited with popularizing the sport around the globe.

In 2000, she was named as one of the Athletes of the 20th Century by the Laureus World Sports Academy.


She has lived in the United States since 1989 and is married to American Olympic gold medal gymnast Bart Conner.

Q&A

The Main differences between Web 1.0, Web 2.0 and Web 3.0:


First, Web 1.0 is consider "Read-only web" - the content creativity was based on the producer.
Web 2.0 is consider "Read-and-Write web" - the content creativity came from users and producers,
and Web 3.0 is consider "Read, Write and execute web", and targets on linked data sets.

Second, Web 1.0 is called "The Hypertext Web" (the basic), Web 2.0 is called "The Community Web"
(for people), and Web 3.0 is known as "The Semantic Web" (for machines).

Third, while Web 1.0 was all about static content - one way publishing of content (without any real
interaction between readers or publishers), Web 2.0 is more about two way communication through
social networking, blogging, wikis and more, and Web 3.0 is undefined, AI and the web learning what
you want and personalize your web experience.


Example of the differences above:

For the query "Hotel in Italy":
In Web 1.0 the results are static, the pages are simple and minimal - documents
with information about hotels in italy. The results may be inaccurate and not suffiecent
for the user.
In web 2.0 the search engines retrieve interactive results that include sharing content,
like blogs - where people shared their opinions about hotels in italy, videos of hotels,
facebook pages of hotels and more social networks.
Web 3.0 is the semantic web and personalize the results to the user, therefore, it may include
recommendations of hotels according to the user's favors and choices in the past - pages
of hotel from the same company that the user already chosen. Another recommendation may be of
other activities in italy, or hotels in other places with geographic proximity.
